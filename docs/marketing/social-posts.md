## Short

## Security-Focused

MoneySiren is for developers who want AI/cloud usage visibility without uploading credentials or raw billing data to another SaaS.

- local SQLite snapshots
- read-only connectors
- telemetry off by default
- fake demo mode
- emergency readiness without provider execution

https://github.com/ztwz11/moneysiren
